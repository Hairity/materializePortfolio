Who am I?

I am front-end web developer from Estonia and I enjoy creating things that live on the internet, whether that be websites, applications, or anything in between. My goal is to always build products that provide pixel-perfect, performant experiences.

Aside from programming my other passion is traveling and food. I have traveled most of Europe and South-East Asia. This picture was taken last year in Vietnam while I was riding a bus. It was a perfect day!

The biggest journey of my life is underway right now, since I found my passion for coding. As self-taught developer some might say it's hard to stay focused, motivated and perhaps fascinated while doing so but lucky for me, these are my characteristics.
